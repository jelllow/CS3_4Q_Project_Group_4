EMPOWERURAL

CAMPAIGN INSIGHT

This campaign is about BRIDGING THE DIGTAL DIVIDE for rural communities. The objective of this campaign is to empower rural communities with access to digital technologies, education, and resources. With these at hand, it is possible to foster economic growth and social inclus in rural communities. Moreover, as scholars currently, we aim to provide service with the help and collaboration of the local government and any other more existing campaigns to further assist us in tackling the projects. Our main goal in this project is to be able to help those in rural areas by providing them the resources they lack. We plan to do this with the help also of donation drives and other more processes in order to collect money, we can also include here the recycling and making use of unused products for possible business and then using that money to help those in need.
